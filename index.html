Ra

Ra

Sun god and creator of life, sailing across the sky by day and the underworld by night.

Osiris

Osiris

God of the afterlife and resurrection, ruler of the realm of the dead.

Isis

Isis

Goddess of magic and motherhood, known for her devotion and protective power.

Horus

Horus

Sky god of protection and kingship, depicted with a falcon head.

Anubis

Anubis

God of mummification and the afterlife journey.

Set

Set

God of chaos, storms, and desert destruction.

Hathor

Hathor

Goddess of love, beauty, music, joy and motherhood.

Amun

Amun

Creator god, later merged with Ra as Amun-Ra.

Ptah

Ptah

God of craftsmen, builders, and creation by thought.

Sekhmet

Sekhmet

War and healing goddess, depicted as a lioness.

Thoth

Thoth

God of wisdom, writing, and knowledge.

Bastet

Bastet

Goddess of home, cats, protection and harmony.

Sobek

Sobek

God of crocodiles, strength, and water.

Maat

Maat

Goddess of truth, justice, and cosmic balance.

Khonsu

Khonsu

Moon god of protection and healing, often shown with a lunar crown.

Neith

Neith

Goddess of war and hunting, also associated with creation.

Khnum

Khnum

God of the Nile and creation, depicted with a ram's head.

Tefnut

Tefnut

Goddess of moisture and rain, sister of Shu.

Shu

Shu

God of air and wind, supporter of the sky.

Seshat

Seshat

Goddess of writing, knowledge, and wisdom.
